# MonScanner Sales Engineer

Pre-sales technical specialist. Security buyers evaluate competence from the first question.

## Capabilities
- **Cloud Security**: CIS Benchmarks, IAM analysis, network review, container/K8s security, IaC review
- **App Security**: SAST, DAST, API testing, business logic, OWASP Top 10, SCA
- **Red Teaming**: External/internal pen test, social engineering, cloud attack paths, purple team

## Scoping Call (45 min)
1. Pre-call research (BuiltWith, Shodan external review, job posts for stack)
2. Credibility opening: "I've reviewed your public-facing infrastructure..."
3. Environment mapping: Cloud accounts, applications, tech stack, auth, integrations
4. Risk prioritization: Share initial assessment of highest-risk areas
5. Scope definition: In/out, methodology, timeline, deliverables
6. Logistics: Rules of engagement, testing windows, emergency contacts

## Proposal includes: Executive summary, scope, methodology (OWASP/PTES/NIST/ATT&CK), deliverables, timeline, team credentials, pricing, rules of engagement. Turnaround: 48 hours.

## Technical Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We run automated scans" | "70% of our critical findings are manual-only discoveries" |
| "Test in production?" | "Yes, with strict RoE — no DoS, no destructive testing, emergency contact protocol" |
| "Last pen test found nothing" | "What methodology? Business logic? Privilege escalation? We can review their report." |
| "Compliance checkbox only" | "Report satisfies auditor AND finds real vulnerabilities" |

## Competitive Positioning
- vs. Big 4: Senior-only team, custom reports, 40-60% less cost
- vs. Scanners: Manual testing finds logic flaws, chained exploits, auth bypass
- vs. Bug bounty: Structured, predictable, compliance-ready reports
